History

PPLV TIMELINE

The beginning of academic year 2012-2013

UCL PPLV was established. First seminar held on 31st Aug 2012 by Delphine Demange was about ‘A Buffered Memory Model for Java’.

November 2014 - Now:

UCL PPLV group runs with a few papers presented / published each month.

December 2015

James Brotherston gave an invited talk, ‘An Introduction to Separation Logic’, at Oracle Labs in Brisbane, Australia. This is the first time that UCL PPLV member was invited to a talk.

May 2016

Peter O'Hearn was awarded the 2016 Gödel Prize.

September 2016

Peter O'Hearn was elected fellow of Royal Academy of Engineering.

June 2012 - October 2014

UCL PPLV recruits group members, and their research papers are presented at the frequency of approximately one per month.

October 2015

Alexandra Silva was awarded a prestigious ERC starting grant (1.5M euros).

February 2016

Alexandra Silva gave an invited tutorial at CMCS 2016.

August 2016

Peter O'Hearn was awarded the 2016 CAV (Computer-Aided Verification) Award.

November 2016

Alexandra Silva was awarded the 2016 Philip Leverhulme Prizes.

March 2017

Alexandra Silva won the EATCS Presburger Award 2017.
